Project Core Modules

A digitally transformed transport system prioritizing student safety, administrative efficiency, and real-time accountability.

Student Module

Device-bound login, real-time attendance dashboard, fee status tracking, and proxy-proof QR boarding with geofence verification.

Driver Module

Automatic GPS heartbeat sync, dynamic secure QR generation, live passenger manifest, and mandatory "Bus Empty" safety checks.

Admin Console

Powerful dashboard for student/fee management, searchable attendance logs with GPS data, and an immutable system audit trail.

Security Protocol

Multi-layer security featuring browser fingerprinting for device binding and rigorous proximity checks using the Haversine formula.

Parent Alerts

Instant (simulated) SMS and email notifications for boarding confirmation and urgent fee reminders for pending accounts.

Incident Reports

A direct complaint center for students to report delays or safety concerns, ensuring swift administrative resolution.

Real-Time Alerts

Parent Notification System

Parents are automatically notified the moment their ward boards the college bus — keeping families informed, reducing anxiety, and ensuring full transparency at every step.

Boarding Confirmation Alert

The moment a student marks attendance via QR scan, parents receive an instant Email & SMS confirming the boarding time, bus number, and date.

Fee Reminder Alerts

Admin can dispatch urgent SMS reminders to parents of students with pending bus fees, directly from the admin panel — one click, instant delivery.

Professional Email Dispatch

Branded HTML emails are sent to the parent's registered address via SMTP, containing full boarding details in a clean, professional format.

Manual Attendance Notifications

Even when a driver manually adds a student to the manifest, the notification pipeline is triggered — ensuring no boarding goes unnoticed.
